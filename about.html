WHO ARE WE

Welcome to SOP Group, your premier provider of cross-border trucking services between Canada and the United States. With years of industry experience, we're dedicated to delivering efficient, reliable, and personalized logistics solutions tailored to your needs. Our team of professionals is committed to ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely, on time, and within budget. Experience the difference with SOP Group and let us elevate your supply chain with our unwavering commitment to excellence.

In today's fast-paced business environment, operational inefficiencies and unexpected complications can significantly impact manufacturers and their supply chain partners.
